Circle Tiler

Upload an image, position it under the circular mask, pick your circle diameter in millimeters, then generate an A4 PDF tiled with your circle.

Tip: drag to move • scroll/slider to zoom • the bright circle is your crop.

Cropping is done locally in your browser; the transparent PNG of your circular crop is sent to the server to build the A4 PDF.

  • The crop export size is based on your chosen mm diameter at ~300 dpi for print quality (capped between 128–4096 px).
  • A4: 210×297 mm; the app tiles circles to fill the page without margins.
